Our Mission

Source Restaurant aims to feed those who experience food insecurity in order to build equitable communities.

Part of a larger social enterprise, our restaurants operate on a buy a meal, give a meal initiative.

With Source, customers are able to give back to their community by ordering their favorite meals.

Our Menu

Our menu items are crafted with locally sourced ingredients. We pride ourselves in our partnerships with neighboring distributors. Community investment is something of a necessity.

Quality matters to us; a great meal for a great cause is what we're all about. That's why we've hand-picked our favorite dishes to share with you each day.
